Lever: margin per job, quote accuracy

  • Operations consistently overrunning by the same delta
  • DFE-TT67F vs TT67F, are customer overrides priced right?

Lever: rework reduction, supplier costs

  • Suppliers causing the most missing-parts blocks

  • Cost per hour of hospital zone vs in-line
Output: supplier scorecards, training-needs map

What changes for the technician

Peter doesn't fill in a paper run sheet anymore. He doesn't look up a drawing in the file cabinet. He doesn't ask Adam what's next.

  • His phone tells him the task, the spec, the estimate, and what's coming
  • Drawing pack, cutting list, BOM are one tap away
  • Clock-in is one button. Clock-out is one button. The system does the maths.
  • Mark Complete asks for a reason if the variance is significant
  • Hospital-zone blocker is one tap. Supervisor sees it immediately.
  • For final QC, the tech runs a checklist, attaches photos, and the system emails the customer DL automatically.

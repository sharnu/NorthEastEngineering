Key decisions
Background — soft mint gradient replaces the warm paper tone. Cards stay pure white for high contrast.
Nav / Sidebar — deep forest green #132b1e replaces near-black. Can stay as topbar or become an icon sidebar (see section 02b).
Accent — forest green #1a7a4d replaces burnt orange. Clock In and primary actions pick this up.
Shadows — green-tinted subtle shadows replace the neutral-gray ones, creating depth without heaviness.
Typography — Fraunces (display) + Inter (sans) + JetBrains Mono unchanged. The font stack is already well-suited.
